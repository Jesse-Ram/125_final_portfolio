AI Tool Acknowledgment

A disclosure of how AI tools were used in the creation of this portfolio, in accordance with course policy.

Tools Used

Two AI tools were used in the creation of this portfolio: Claude (Anthropic) and ChatGPT (OpenAI).

Website Development

I have a background in web development and built this portfolio using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The design decisions, structure, and content organization are my own. Claude was used to accelerate parts of the development process, specifically generating boilerplate code, formatting HTML sections, and helping debug layout issues. The overall architecture and all content decisions were made by me.

Writing and Content

For the written content in this portfolio, AI tools were used in two ways. First, as a writing aid to help structure and draft sections of the reflection after I had developed my own ideas and arguments. The positions, examples, and connections to course materials are mine. Second, as a conversation partner to help me work through concepts from the readings that I found difficult to articulate. Some of the ideas covered in this course were genuinely hard to explain clearly, and talking through them in a back and forth conversation helped me understand them well enough to write about them in my own words. That process is similar to talking through a concept with a classmate or a TA, and I want to be transparent that AI played that role at times during this semester.

All work submitted for grading reflects my own understanding and analysis. AI was not used to generate arguments I did not understand or to substitute for engaging with the course material.
